Stefanos Papanikolaou

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Stefanos Papanikolaou, a 21-year-old creative virtuoso,
stands out as a remarkably talented graphic designer with
a keen eye for fashion aesthetics. His design prowess
extends beyond the conventional, weaving together
innovation and style to create visually stunning
works of art. Stefanos' passion for graphic design is
only rivaled by his love for exploration; constantly
on the move, he draws inspiration from his travels to
infuse a diverse cultural perspective into his projects.
With a fresh and dynamic approach, Stefanos Papanikolaou
is a rising star in the world of graphic design,
leaving an indelible mark on the intersection of
creativity and contemporary style.

Stelios Vasileiou

Co-Founder & Public Relations

At just 21 years old, Stelios Vasiliou emerges as a
prodigious talent in the realm of music production,
showcasing an innate ability to craft compelling and
immersive soundscapes. Beyond his musical prowess,
Stelios brings a unique blend of experience from the
luxury industry, adding a touch of sophistication
to his creative endeavors. Having worked in the
vibrant atmosphere of Mykonos, he has collaborated
with world-famous personalities, seamlessly blending
his musical expertise with the glamorous backdrop of
the international elite. Stelios Vasiliou stands at
the intersection of youth and sophistication,
defining a new era of sound in the global music scene.
